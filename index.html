José Rizal University is thrilled to announce that we have been Certified™ by Great Place To Work® for 2024!

This recognition is a testament to our commitment to fostering a positive and engaging workplace culture, as evidenced by our own employees’ feedback. An impressive 91% of our staff have affirmed that José Rizal University is a great place to work, with 94% specifically highlighting that we provide a physically safe working environment—exceeding the average rating of U.S. companies.

“Great Place To Work Certification is a highly coveted achievement that requires consistent and intentional dedication to the overall employee experience,” says Sarah Lewis-Kulin, the Vice President of Global Recognition at Great Place To Work. She emphasizes that Certification is the sole official recognition earned by the real-time feedback of employees regarding their company culture. “By successfully earning this recognition, it is evident that José Rizal University stands out as one of the top companies to work for, providing a great workplace environment for its employees.”

This certification not only honors the efforts we put into maintaining a supportive and enriching work environment but also motivates us to continue our journey toward excellence. We extend our heartfelt thanks to all our employees for their invaluable contributions and for making José Rizal University a truly great place to work.
